At RMP Farms, we believe in fostering strong partnerships with local farmers through our comprehensive contract farming program. With over 800 contract farms, we provide the resources, support, and expertise necessary to ensure mutual success and the highest standards of poultry production. Discover how partnering with us can help you achieve your farming goals and contribute to a sustainable agricultural community.

Why Partner with RMP Farms?

1. Initial Consultation

We begin by conducting an initial consultation to understand your farming needs and goals. Our team will assess your current operations and identify areas for improvement, ensuring that we tailor our support to your specific requirements.

2. Agreement and Planning

Once we have a clear understanding of your needs, we will draft a contract farming agreement that outlines the terms and conditions of our partnership. This agreement will detail the resources, support, and expectations for both parties, ensuring transparency and mutual understanding.

5. Harvest and Sale

At the end of the farming cycle, we will assist you with the harvest and sale of your produce. Our established market connections and competitive pricing ensure that you receive fair and timely payments, providing financial stability and rewarding your hard work.

3. Resource Provision

After the agreement is finalized, we will provide you with the necessary resources, including high-quality broiler chicks, premium feed, and farming equipment. Our team will also offer training and guidance on how to use these resources effectively.

4. Ongoing Support

Throughout the farming cycle, our team of experts will provide ongoing support and guidance. We will conduct regular visits to monitor your progress, offer advice, and address any challenges you may encounter.
