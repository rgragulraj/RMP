What We Do

At RMP Farms, our dedication to excellence is evident in every aspect of our operations. From breeding top-quality broiler chicks to producing premium feed, we cover the entire spectrum of poultry farming with a focus on quality, sustainability, and innovation.

Chickens

Our broiler farming operations focus on raising healthy, high-quality chickens sold primarily to traders. We provide optimal living conditions, including spacious environments, clean water, and balanced diets, ensuring superior meat quality. We adhere to strict animal welfare standards, minimizing stress and promoting natural behaviors. Advanced farming techniques and regular health checks ensure our chickens meet the highest industry standards for quality and safety.

Egg

Our egg production processes ensure the best parent and broiler eggs. Our hens receive optimal nutrition and care in modern facilities, resulting in high-quality eggs. We use innovative methods to maximize egg quality and quantity, including precision feeding and environmental controls. Each egg undergoes rigorous quality checks before being used for hatching or sold to customers.

Chick

Our breeding and hatching processes produce top-quality broiler chicks. We maintain a robust breeding program and state-of-the-art hatcheries to ensure healthy chicks. High-quality parent hens and precise incubation conditions result in strong, healthy chicks. From day one, our chicks receive optimal nutrition and a safe, clean environment, promoting healthy growth and development.

Feed

Our feed production ensures our poultry receives the best nutrition. We operate three feed mills and manage two conversion mills, producing premium feed and selling the excess to other farmers. Our feed is formulated for optimal poultry health and growth, using high-quality ingredients and precise formulations. We use environmentally friendly methods in our feed production processes, minimizing waste and reducing our environmental footprint.
